FAQ.

What if it rains on an outdoor shoot day?

I work with $20,000+ in sensitive, non-waterproof equipment. Any rain in the forecast moves the shoot indoors immediately. Usually the field house or event center, depending on team size.

What is "golden hour" and why does it matter?

For outdoor sports, the best window is the hour before sunset through the hour after. The light is dramatic and flattering. Booking early helps lock that window in.

What's in the Athlete Portal and pre-shoot form?

Each athlete picks poses, prop ideas, and references ahead of time, personalized to who they are. Coming in prepared is the single biggest factor in whether the photos land.

Why does warm-up matter on a photoshoot?

It takes a few photos before most athletes loosen up on camera. Warm-up is often what separates a viral shot from a regular one. Photos will still come out good without it. They just tend to be less expressive of who the athlete actually is, and less interesting overall.

The lower-photo tiers leave less room for warm-up, which is why fewer dependable poses work best there. Higher tiers build in room to push into action shots and more complex setups.

How does All-Access actually work?

Each athlete gets as many individual and group photos as they want within their slot. Most land around 10–20 photos counting both individuals and groups together. Groups are unlimited inside the team's full time block.

How is pricing structured?

Tiers don't scale linearly because different packages take different amounts of shooting and editing time. Group photos scale at a smaller rate than individuals because each group photo covers multiple athletes in one frame.

Teams of 45 or more get a sliding volume discount that bottoms out around 60 athletes. Preseason shoots add a flat $100 travel fee.

When and how do athletes get their photos?

Most galleries are ready within 1.5 months, often closer to 2 to 3 weeks. Anyone who needs the photos (athletes, coaches, athletics staff) gets an email with the gallery link and a download code. The link is viewable. The download code is what gates downloads.

Where do these photos end up beyond the team?

On video boards, in media guides, across social, and in national coverage when it comes. They don't expire after the season.
